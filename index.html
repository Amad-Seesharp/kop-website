What is MyPB? Apa itu MyPB?

Secure Support System for Inmates Sistem Sokongan Selamat untuk Penghuni

Malaysian Prospek Buying (MyPB) replaces traditional cash transfers with a transparent, digital voucher system. Malaysian Prospek Buying (MyPB) menggantikan pemindahan tunai tradisional dengan sistem baucar digital yang telus.

Secure & Compliant Selamat & Patuh

Fully compliant with prison financial regulations, ensuring every sen reaches its destination safely. Mematuhi sepenuhnya peraturan kewangan penjara, memastikan setiap sen sampai ke destinasi dengan selamat.

Instant Processing Pemprosesan Segera

Digital vouchers are processed in real-time, reducing the waiting period for inmates to receive support. Baucar digital diproses dalam masa nyata, mengurangkan tempoh menunggu penghuni menerima bantuan.

Easy Access Akses Mudah

Manage your account, track history, and send vouchers from any device, anywhere in Malaysia. Urus akaun, jejak sejarah, dan hantar baucar dari mana-mana peranti, di mana sahaja di Malaysia.

How It Works Cara Ia Berfungsi

Sending support to your loved ones is simple and transparent. Menghantar sokongan kepada orang tersayang adalah mudah dan telus.

1

Create Account Daftar Akaun

Sign up using your IC number and verify your identity to ensure a secure connection to the facility. Daftar menggunakan No. KP dan sahkan identiti anda untuk sambungan selamat ke fasiliti.

2

Verify Inmate Details Sahkan Maklumat Penghuni

Locate your family member by inputting their Inmate Number and selecting their current prison facility. Cari ahli keluarga dengan memasukkan Nombor Banduan dan memilih lokasi penjara mereka.

3

Send Voucher Hantar Baucar

Purchase a digital voucher. The amount is credited to the inmate's trust account for commissary use. Beli baucar digital. Jumlah dikreditkan ke akaun amanah penghuni untuk kegunaan komisar.

Frequently Asked Questions Soalan Lazim

What information do I need to register? Apakah maklumat yang diperlukan untuk mendaftar?
You will need your NRIC (MyKad), a valid mobile phone number for TAC verification, and the Inmate Number of the person you wish to support. Anda memerlukan Kad Pengenalan (MyKad), nombor telefon bimbit yang sah untuk pengesahan TAC, dan Nombor Banduan orang yang ingin anda bantu.
Are there limits on the voucher amount? Adakah terdapat had jumlah baucar?
Yes, spending limits are set by the prison authorities. Our system will notify you if a purchase exceeds the monthly quota for a specific inmate. Ya, had perbelanjaan ditetapkan oleh pihak berkuasa penjara. Sistem akan memaklumkan jika pembelian melebihi kuota bulanan bagi penghuni tersebut.
What payment methods do you accept? Apakah kaedah pembayaran yang diterima?
We accept all major Malaysian online banking options via FPX, as well as Visa and Mastercard credit/debit cards. Kami menerima semua pilihan perbankan dalam talian utama Malaysia melalui FPX, serta kad kredit/debit Visa dan Mastercard.
How long does it take for the funds to arrive? Berapa lamakah masa untuk dana sampai?
Transactions are processed immediately. However, it may take 24-48 hours for the prison administration to reflect the balance in the inmate's ledger for spending. Transaksi diproses serta-merta. Walau bagaimanapun, ia mungkin mengambil masa 24-48 jam untuk pentadbiran penjara mengemas kini baki dalam lejar penghuni.
